Bio

Hi, I'm Enoch Satrom. I'm getting a major in mechanical engineering with minors in robotics and computer science from NDSU, graduating spring of 2024. My senior design project was finished last semester, you can see our electric moped pictured to the left. I have a job lined up with ReechCraft in Fargo once I graduate - we make elevating work platforms of a few different varieties, and even if it doesn't apply much of what I've learned about robotics or computer science, it's still a fun gig. I plan to stay with them for the forseeable future and learn more with people who have big aspirations and really know what they're doing.
